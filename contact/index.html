My social accounts

There are two kinds of relationships on social networks.

  • Asymmetric means you can follow me even if I don't follow you back.
  • Symmetric means we can't connect unless we both agree to the connection.

I prefer networks with asymmetric relationships; O'Reilly nicely articulates their advantages.
